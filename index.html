NASA's InSight Mars lander collects continuous measurements with regards to Weather on the surface of Mars at Elysium Planitia, near Mars' equator.

The Near Earth Object Web Service or N.E.O.W.S provides deep data pertaining to Asteroids which come into close proximity to Earth. The API is maintained by the SpaceRocks Team.

Data relating to: Comet Close Approaches, Fireballs, Mission Design, NHATS, Scout and Sentry.

NASA provides free and open access to its Exoplanet Archive, a collection of planets outside our Solar System.

NASA runs Astronomy Picture Of Day, which is exqactly as the name describes: an Astronomy Photo each day since June 16 1995.
